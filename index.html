Paris | 2024

Form before noise

Philosophy

Founded in 2024 in Paris, **EBLAN** was created as a response to visual noise and unnecessary complexity.

The brand focuses on form, material, and silence. Clothing designed to last, to be worn without explanation, and to age with dignity.

Positioning

Quiet luxury. Minimal fashion. EBLAN does not follow trends. It creates essential garments for people who value restraint and clarity.

"Garments that speak through silence"

Visual Code

Our palette is inspired by stone, shadows, and urban architecture.
